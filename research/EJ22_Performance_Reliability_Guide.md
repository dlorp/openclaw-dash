## ðŸ”§ Stage 1: Budget-Friendly Daily Driver Mods ($500-1500)

### Proven Bolt-On Gains

**1. Intake & Exhaust Flow ($300-800)**
- **Headers:** Unequal length 4-2-1 stainless steel headers (~$200-400)
  - Real-world: Australian owner reported noticeable improvement
  - Japanese shops (TOMEI, HKS) recommend equal-length for turbo builds, unequal for NA torque
- **Cold Air Intake:** Pod filter + adapter (~$50-150)
  - Ford Explorer V6 example: +19 HP from pod filter alone
  - EJ22 realistic: +5-10 HP
- **Cat-Back Exhaust:** Free-flowing muffler (~$150-300)
  - Avoid too large diameter (reduces low-end torque)
  - 2.25" - 2.5" optimal for NA EJ22

**Expected Gains:** +10-15 HP, improved throttle response, better sound

**2. ECU Tuning/Piggyback ($300-500)**
- Aftermarket ECU if factory is locked
- **Realistic NA gains:** ~15% power increase with supporting mods
- **Critical:** Must include knock protection
- Japanese tuners emphasize proper setup over peak numbers

**Total Stage 1 Investment:** ~$600-1300  
**Total Gains:** +15-20 HP, improved drivability

## ðŸ”¨ Stage 2: Enthusiast Modifications ($1500-3500)

**1. Camshafts ($400-800 + $300-600 install)**
- **Fast road cams:** Best for street driving
  - Increase mid-high RPM power
  - Minor low-end torque loss acceptable for spirited driving
- **Avoid competition cams for daily use:**
  - Lumpy idle
  - Poor low-end response
  - Japanese mechanics warn: "traffic driving will be miserable"

**2. Port & Polish Head ($800-1500)**
- Remove flow restrictions
- Smooth turbulence
- Best when combined with larger valves

**3. High-Flow Fuel System ($400-800)**
- Upgraded injectors
- Performance fuel pump
- Required for Stage 2+ power levels

**Expected Gains:** +25-35 HP over stock  
**Total Stage 2 Investment:** ~$2700-3900

## ðŸš€ Stage 3: Serious Performance ($5000-10000+)

**Forced Induction (Turbo/Supercharger)**
- Most cost-effective power per dollar at high HP levels
- NA-to-turbo conversion requires:
  - Forged internals (pistons, rods)
  - Upgraded head gasket (RCM 0.78mm performance gasket)
  - Standalone ECU
  - Intercooler, piping, BOV
  - Fuel system overhaul
  - Oil system upgrades

**Japanese Tuner Kits:**
- **TOMEI ARMS T390M/T440M Single Scroll Turbo Kit** (high-end, proven)
- **HKS GT Series** (widely supported)

**Expected Gains:** +80-150 HP (conservative tuning for reliability)
