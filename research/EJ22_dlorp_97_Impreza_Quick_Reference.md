## üöó Driving Tips for Longevity

1. **Warm-up before spirited driving**
   - 5 minutes minimum
   - Japanese tuners emphasize this for turbo AND NA

2. **Cool-down after hard use**
   - Idle 30-60 seconds before shutdown
   - Prevents heat soak

3. **Monitor coolant temp**
   - Evoscan real-time logging
   - Watch for overheating trends

4. **Regular spirited driving is GOOD**
   - Keeps carbon buildup down
   - Italian tune-up works

5. **Quality fluids matter**
   - Synthetic oil (5W-30)
   - OEM coolant or equivalent
   - Don't cheap out
